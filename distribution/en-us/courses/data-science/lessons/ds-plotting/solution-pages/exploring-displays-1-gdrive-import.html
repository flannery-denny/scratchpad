Box plots are constructed from 1 column(s), and show quantitative data. What do you think this display tells us? The range of quantitative values in the bottom quarter, top quarter, and the middle quarters. This tells us something about the spread of the data

Histograms are constructed from 1 column(s), and show quantitative data. What do you think this display tells us? How many values in a quantitative column fall into equally-sized bins. This tells us something about the spread of the data
